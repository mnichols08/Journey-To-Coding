Pontoon Tours

Spend an hour relaxing on our pontoon boat while our captain shows you around Deep Creek Lake

Come aboard our 24 foot pontoon boat for a 1 hour tour of Deep Creek Lake. Sit back, relax, and enjoy beautiful Deep Creek Lake as your Captain pilots the boat. If you would like our Captain would be happy to share some history and local knowledge of Deep Creek Lake. Rest assured this is not a speed boat. Reservations recommended. Lifejackets are required for anyone under the age of 13 and anyone unable to swim. Call for Special Charters.

Our Deep Creek Lake Pontoon Boat tours leave at 1 pm and 3 pm.

If you look at Deep Creek Lake Pontoon Boat Rentals you will find that our tours are very economical compared to renting a pontoon boat for the day on Deep Creek Lake. We also don't have any tests or license required. Our only requirement is that you allow us to show you a great time on Deep Creek Lake.

Even in the summer it can be cool on Deep Creek Lake if the wind is blowing so don't forget a windbreaker or jacket. Of course you will want to bring a camera. If we happen to get caught in a rainstorm there is minimal cover on the boat and you may get a little wet. Everyone under the age of 13 must wear a lifejacket while we are underway.

To get to the boat from our store you will cross Route 219, go down a small hill and onto the docks.  We will be happy to offer assistance to anyone who needs it.

We think you are going to love our Boat Tours on Deep Creek Lake.

Prices do not include taxes or fees.

2019 Deep Creek Lake Dock Slip Rentals

Dock Slip Rental

Dock Slip Amenities:

  • Boats are checked on a regular basis by our maintenance staff.
  • We have lakefront parking making quick access to your boat.
  • There are also several picnic tables in our wooded oak grove fronting Deep Creek Lake.
  • Dock Assistant during busy times to assist docking

Young and old alike enjoy fishing for the many species of fish on our lakefront including smallmouth bass, largemouth bass, trout, sunfish, pickerel. Kayak rentals, standup paddleboard rentals, Pontoon Boat Tours, and bike rentals are also available onsite. During busy periods we employ a dock assistant who is available to help you load/unload/dock. These dock assistants work on tips.

2019 Seasonal Dock Slip Rates; Our Waiting List is pretty full for 2019 but please contact us to be placed on the list for 2020.  There is a non-refundable $30 fee to be placed on the Waiting List. Click here to sign up for the list.

The docks that are conveniently located at the Traders Landing Retail Complex on Garrett Highway (locally known as RT 219). The complex includes High Mountain Sports, Brenda's Pizzeria, Traders Coffee House, Grand Central Furniture, and Subway Sub Shop.

Short Term Dock Rentals are for a 24 Hour Period from 3PM to 3PM.

We do not have parking for trailers. Contact Double G RV Park in McHenry, they offer trailer parking for the week - 301-387-5481.  We also suggest using the boat ramp at Deep Creek Lake State Park.  There is a fee at both places.

Our dock rentals are limited to one boat or one personal watercraft (jetski).  If you have tubes or kayaks they must be stored so they do not hit another boat or obstruct the dock walkways.

All rentals are non-refundable.

If you do not prepay online we only accept cash or checks in the store if we have slips available.

After you reserve your slip online you must sign in with High Mountain Sports when you come to use your dock slip and sign a liability release. High Mountain Sports is open 9-7 seven days a week. When you sign in we will assign you a dock and a Traders Dock Permit to display on your boat. We are not responsible for any damage done to your vessel. When you check in you will be asked to fill out a Liability Waiver.  You will need your basic boat information.

Questions?  Email us at docks@highmountainsports.com.
