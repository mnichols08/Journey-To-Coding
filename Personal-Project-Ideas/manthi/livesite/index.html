Hey there! My name is Manthana (Manthi for short). I am a visual artist.

In May 2015 I obtained a B.F.A. from Radford University.

I love experiencing with all kinds of art ever since I was young. Throughout my years, I've dabbled in various art mediums such as drawing, watercolor painting, photography, plush making and many more.

I'm a quiet person in nature so I let my art speak.

I am motivated and meticulous when it comes to creating a project. I would call myself a big perfectionist. I have an exceptionally high attention to detail. I tend to not stop until what I'm making satisfies my eyes.

I'm continuing to grow as an artist and looking for opportunities.

Please don't hesitate to get in touch with me.
